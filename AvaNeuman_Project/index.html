OUR STORY
At Coffee Cube, our story is steeped in the aroma of passion and brewed with dedication. Nestled near the iconic Prašná Brána in the heart of Staré Město and with another welcoming spot in the bustling area of Můstek, we’ve built more than just coffee shops—we’ve cultivated communities. Our journey began with a simple mission: to craft exceptional coffee while creating warm, inviting spaces where everyone feels at home.

From sourcing ethically-grown beans to perfecting the art of the pour, every detail matters. Each cup tells a story—of the farmers who grow with care, the baristas who pour with skill, and the customers who bring it all to life. Whether you’re seeking a cozy corner to savor your favorite brew, a space to connect with friends, or just a moment of solace in your busy day, Coffee Cube is here for you. Come be part of our story, one sip at a time.
